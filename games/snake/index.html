How to Play

Use the arrow keys to control the snake:

  • ↑ - Move Up
  • ↓ - Move Down
  • ← - Move Left
  • → - Move Right

Press Space bar to pause/resume the game.

Eat the food to grow longer and earn points. Avoid hitting the walls or yourself!
